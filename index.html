Who we are

Built for people already in the water.

The Fleet is a crew out of Point Judith chasing whatever the season gives us — yellowfin and mahi offshore, striped bass on the troll, threshers and makos when they push in, and tog on the end of a spear. We love sharing the experience and pulling more people into an outdoor life.
